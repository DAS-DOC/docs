## **Checklist of Required Documents**

Here’s a detailed list of documents required, categorized by business type. Gathering all relevant documents beforehand will help avoid delays during the onboarding process.

<Accordion title="Partner (Single)">
- **ID Card**
- **Screenshot of Bank Account Details**
- **Email Address**
- **Phone Number**
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Partner (Entity)">
- **History of All Matters**
- **ID Card for Boss and UBO (Ultimate Beneficial Owner)**
- **Screenshot of Bank Account Details**
- **Shareholder Registry**
- **Proof of Address**
- **Website URL**
- **Email Address**
- **Phone Number**
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Partner (Sole Proprietor)">
- **History of All Matters**
- **Business Certificate**
- **ID Card**
- **Screenshot of Bank Account Details**
- **Proof of Address (If there is registered office)**
- **Email Address**
- **Phone Number**
</Accordion>


## **Final Steps**

Please ensure that all required documents are submitted during the account creation process to avoid delays. Once you’ve gathered all the necessary documents, you can proceed to the [Create an Account](https://me.paymentoptions.com/onboarding/sign-up) form.

## **How Pay by Link Works**

Pay by Link provides a flexible way for customers to make payments remotely. Once you generate a link within the gateway, simply share it with your customer, who can then complete their transaction on a secure payment page.

## **Getting Started with Pay by Link**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accessing Pay by Link">
    Log in to the gateway, navigate to the Pay by Link section, and view your existing links or create a new one.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Creating a New Link">
    Click to create a new link, then add items to your cart (product name, quantity, unit price). Set an expiry date and generate the link.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activating the Link">
    Once you generate the link, it becomes active and can be used by your customer.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sharing the Link">
    Copy the generated link and share it with your customer via email, SMS, or any messaging app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deactivating the Link">
    If a link is no longer needed, you can deactivate it from the Pay by Link list, setting its status to inactive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Customize each link with an expiry date and item details for a better user experience.</Tip>

## **How Customers Use Pay by Link**

Once shared, your customer can open the link from the communication method you used (email, SMS, etc.) and complete the payment on the secure page.

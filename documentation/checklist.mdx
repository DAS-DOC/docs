## **Checklist of Required Documents**

Here's a detailed list of documents that you'll need to provide, categorized by business type. Make sure to gather all relevant documents to avoid delays during the onboarding process.

<Accordion title="Business Owner / UBO (Ultimate Beneficial Owner)">
  - **Name**
  - **Personal Address**
  - **Date of Birth**
  - **Face Image**
  - **Gender**
  - **ID CARD**: Passport, My Number, or Driver's License
  
  <Note>
    Download our [ID Verification Form](/downloads/id-verification-form.pdf) to submit your ID information.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Person of Contact">
  - **Name**
  - **Personal Address**
  - **Date of Birth**
  - **Face Image**
  - **Gender**
  - **ID CARD**: Passport, My Number, or Driver's License
  - **Email Address**
  - **Phone Number**
  
  <Note>
    Download our [Contact Information Form](/downloads/contact-info-form.pdf) to submit your details.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Sole Proprietor">
  - **Business Certificate**: Proof of business registration.
  - **ID Card**
  - **Screenshot of Bank Account Details**: Must include bank account number and name.
  - **Business Address**: If using a rental address, provide proof of rental.
  - **Website URL**
  - **Email Address**
  - **Phone Number**
  
  <Note>
    Download our [Sole Proprietor Documentation Package](/downloads/sole-proprietor-package.zip) which includes all necessary forms.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Entity (For Registered Businesses)">
  - **Company History**: Full business background and relevant matters.
  - **ID Card for Boss and UBO**: Identification details as mentioned for Business Owners.
  - **Screenshot of Bank Account Details**: Must include bank account number and name.
  - **Shareholder Registry**
  - **Proof of Business Address**
  - **Website URL**
  - **Email Address**
  - **Phone Number**
  
  <Note>
    Download our [Business Entity Documentation Package](/downloads/business-entity-package.zip) which includes all necessary forms.
  </Note>
</Accordion>


## **Final Steps**

Please ensure that all required documents are submitted during the account creation process to avoid delays. Once you've gathered all the necessary documents, you can proceed to the [Create an Account](https://me.paymentoptions.com/onboarding/sign-up) form.
